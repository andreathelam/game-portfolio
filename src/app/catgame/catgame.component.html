OVERVIEW

Embark on an adventure as a mushroom cat in this 2D platformer, navigating through diverse landscapes while evading flying predators. Your mission: to catch precious mushrooms, all while harboring dreams of discovering the elusive and enchanted super shroom.

I started this project because I wanted to learn how to build the user interface of a settings menu through Unreal’s UI Designer. It quickly evolved into a full-on game development project, as I undertook the creation of all art assets and the implementation of the mechanics. This is very much still a work-in-progress, and I share my future plans for this game at the bottom of this page.

Designing the Map


I focused on layering the background and foreground elements in order to add dimension to the game environment. To create a sense of boundless wonder, I wanted each tile to pieced together seamlessly without feeling repetitive. As the player journeys through the world, they’ll notice that higher aptitudes reveal a lighter environment surrounded by clouds, while descending into the lower level unveils a darker environment. Foliage, such as grass and flowers, were added to bring more detail into the landscape.

For the interactive elements, players will discover mushrooms scattered throughout with the final mushroom hidden in a chest. Flying birds act as obstacles that the player must dodge and navigate to progress.

Implementation in Unreal

Building this game from scratch was a challenge, but I found tutorial videos from Youtube channel, Cobra Code, to be extremely helpful with the initial setup and foundation. I also utilized the Common UI plugin to create global styles and reusable widgets. With a combination of watching videos and a lot of googling, I was able to complete a “first playable” of my game.

What's Next?

My future goals include implementing functionality to the settings menu so players can adjust the game to fit their needs and adding an in-game store where players can exchange their collected mushrooms to acquire cosmetics items for their cat character. Along with that, I have a list of quality of life improvements and bug fixes that include additional sound and video effects as well as optimizing load times for a smoother gaming experience.
